The Problem We're Solving

The education gap is real — even in America.

It's easy to think the education access problem is somewhere else. It isn't. In the United States today, a student's chance of attending a top university is still strongly predicted by their parents' income — not their raw ability or drive.

First-generation students, low-income families, and underserved communities lack access to the tutors, test prep, essay coaches, and enrichment programs that wealthy peers take for granted. The playing field was never level.

Globally, the picture is starker. Across Sub-Saharan Africa, Southeast Asia, and South Asia, hundreds of millions of brilliant young people simply have no path to quality instruction — not because they don't want it, but because the infrastructure was never built for them.

OneTeach is our answer. One minute at a time, we're putting the world's knowledge in everyone's pocket.
