Our Mission

Education is a
right, not a privilege.

Two billion people lack meaningful access to quality education. Not because they aren't curious or capable — but because books are expensive, schools are overcrowded, and internet data is scarce.

OneTeach was built to change that. Our one-minute lessons are lightweight, low-bandwidth, and free. Optimized for the phone in your pocket, wherever you are in the world.

We believe that a great explanation should be available to a student in Lagos just as easily as one in London. Knowledge belongs to everyone.
